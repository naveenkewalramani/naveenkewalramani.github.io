Neuro Linguistic Programming Workshop


A coach, counsellor and trainer of Neuro Linguistic Programming, Ridhima Dua is founder of iFlame Consulting. She is a Corporate Trainer & Leadership Coach, Certified NFNLP, US Master and Basic Practitioner & Trainer. With a number of other qualifications she has trained 4000+ people and is moving ahead with a mission to transform 1 million people and change their lives.

ABSTRACT:

Neuro Linguistic Programming (NLP) is focused on making a person to reach greater depths of his/her inner self. NLP is a nutshell with each letter having its significance:
Neuro “The Mind” - your thinking process, the way you utilize your five senses to understand your surrounding.
Linguistic “The Language” - the way you use words and language, both verbal and non- verbal and how it influence people.
Programming “The Behavior” - how the above two factors affect your body and behavior.

It is a program that helps a person get a clear vision, purpose of life and achieve success in whatever he/she does by overcoming the limiting beliefs, habits and behaviors of life. It involves some core inventories like:

Power of your unconscious- a freeway to change.
Quality of your thoughts- Sub modalities.
Running your own brain- Neurochemistry
Building new beliefs- The structure of certainty.
Timeline- How you mentally code time.

Once you go through this program, you are sure to emerge as a new being with empowering beliefs, love for self and a skilled communication powers with self and others and much more energy and confidence. Amazing! Fantastic! Are one of the natural reactions of trainees. So get ready to experience a positive reformation.
