Registration Steps


Phase 1


  • The first step toward AISYWC’17 involves filling up of the Registration form. We request Student, Young Professional and Women in engineering delegates to fill the form, as the selection for further rounds will depend upon the entries received. .

Phase 2


  • Shortlisted delegates from the first phase would be invited for the second round of registration. The selected candidates will be required to make the payment as per their category. After the submission of the registration fee, an event ticket would be generated and a confirmation mail would be sent.
