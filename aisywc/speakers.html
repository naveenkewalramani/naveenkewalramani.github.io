Prof Nandi has great academic vision which he has demonstrated time and again and which he had acquired in working with three of the greatest countries of the world, USA, Russia and China. Dr. Sharmila Mande
Head of the Bio-Sciences R&D activities
TCS Innovation Labs


Dr. Sharmila Mande is Chief Scientist and Head of the Bio-Sciences R&D activities at TCS Innovation Labs (Pune), India. She also leads the ‘Digital Health and Personalized Medicine’ program at TCS. She has more than 30 years of researh experience in Life Sciences domain.

She received her PhD degree in the year 1991 in Physics from Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore. She had her post-doctoral training at University of Groningen, The Netherlands and University of Washington, Seattle, USA. She continued her research at Institute of Microbial Technology (IMTech) and Post Graduate Institute of Medical Education and Research (PGIMER) in Chandigarh, before joining TCS in 2001 to start the Life Sciences R&D activities at TCS. She is on the board of one of the Tata group companies, Advinus Therapeutics.

During her sixteen years at TCS, she has successfully built a group of Bioinformaticians, and trained many of them, including engineers, mathematicians, physicists and biologists in this field. Her main areas of research include microbiome analytics, biomarker discovery for early diagnosis of diseases/disorders, algorithm development for analyzing large scale biological data and applying the same to understand human health. She has a number of projects in collaboration with several renowned National/International academic institutes as well as hospitals. The focus of these projects is on diseases/disorders like malnourishment, diabetes and tuberculosis.
